Heart Disease Analysis

Data driven insights to understand heart disease risks

Heart Disease

Heart disease is a major global health problem that impacts millions of people every year. Early detection and lifestyle changes play a crucial role in reducing risk and improving survival rates. Data-driven analysis helps in identifying key patterns and risk factors.

Dashboards

Three scenarios for heart disease analysis

Scenario 1: Doctor View

Analysis focused on demographic and medical risk factors to help cardiologists identify high-risk patients.

Scenario 2: Government View

Regional and lifestyle-based analysis to support public health policy and preventive programs.

Scenario 3: Patient View

Personal health dashboard showing BMI, sleep, activity and lifestyle risk indicators.
